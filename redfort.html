Red Fort

Delhi, India

The Red Fort is a historic fort in Delhi that served as the main residence of the Mughal emperors. It is known for its massive enclosing walls of red sandstone.

Key Facts

  • Construction began in 1638 and was completed in 1648.
  • A UNESCO World Heritage Site since 2007.
  • Hosts the annual Independence Day celebrations in India.
